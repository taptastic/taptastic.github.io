=========== README ===========
HOW TO PLAY TAPTASTIC:
1) Upload it: Choose your own song in MP3 format and upload it to our game's website
2) Play it first: Our game requires some time to interpret the data, so let it run through the song first time
3) Make it: Just press the "Make Song" button and you're all set! Have fun.

Control: D-Pad (Up / Down / Left / Right)
Goal: Do your best to attain the highest score that you can reach with your very own song.
You can even challenge your friends for a small competition.

Link to our game: https://taptastic.github.io/
